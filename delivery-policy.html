Delivery Policy

Delivery Areas

Dhen's Kitchen currently delivers to the following areas:

  • Manila
  • Quezon City
  • Makati
  • Pasig
  • Mandaluyong
  • San Juan
  • Taguig
  • Parañaque
  • Pasay

For areas outside Metro Manila, please contact us to check if delivery is available.

Delivery Fees

Our delivery fees are as follows:

  • Manila - ₱100
  • Quezon City - ₱150
  • Makati - ₱150
  • Pasig - ₱200
  • Mandaluyong - ₱150
  • San Juan - ₱150
  • Taguig - ₱200
  • Parañaque - ₱250
  • Pasay - ₱200

Free delivery for orders above ₱3,000 within Metro Manila.

Delivery Schedule

We deliver during the following hours:

  • Monday to Friday: 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM
  • Saturday and Sunday: 9:00 AM - 9:00 PM
  • Holidays: 10:00 AM - 7:00 PM

Please note that delivery times may vary depending on traffic conditions and order volume.

Delivery Time

Estimated delivery times:

  • Regular menu items: 60-90 minutes from order confirmation
  • Cakes and desserts: May require 24-48 hours advance notice
  • Customized orders: As agreed upon during order placement

You will receive a confirmation call or message when your order is dispatched.

Order Tracking

You can track your order by:

  • Logging into your account on our website
  • Contacting our customer service at (02) 8123-4567
  • Responding to the confirmation message you received

We will provide updates on your order status and estimated delivery time.

Delivery Conditions

Please note the following delivery conditions:

  • Someone must be present at the delivery address to receive the order
  • Our delivery personnel will wait for a maximum of 10 minutes
  • If no one is available to receive the order, it will be returned to our store and you will need to arrange for redelivery (additional fees may apply)
  • Please provide accurate and complete delivery address information
  • Contact number must be reachable during delivery

For any questions about our delivery policy, please contact us.
