Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

You can place an order through our website by visiting the Order page, selecting your items, and proceeding to checkout. You can also call us directly at (123) 456-7890 to place an order over the phone.

For large orders or special requests, we recommend placing your order at least 48 hours in advance.

What are your delivery hours and fees?

We deliver from 10 AM to 8 PM, seven days a week. Delivery fees are as follows:

  • Within 5 miles: $5.00
  • 5-10 miles: $8.00
  • Over 10 miles: Please call for availability and pricing

Free delivery is available for orders over $50 within a 5-mile radius.

Do you cater for events?

Yes, we offer catering services for all types of events including weddings, birthdays, corporate functions, and more. Our catering menu includes a wide range of options that can be customized to suit your needs.

For catering inquiries, please contact us at catering@dhenskitchen.com or call (123) 456-7891 to speak with our catering specialist.

How far in advance should I place my order?

For regular menu items, we recommend placing your order at least 2 hours in advance to ensure availability. For custom cakes and large orders, we require:

  • Custom cakes: 3-5 days notice
  • Catering orders: 1 week notice
  • Special occasion cakes (weddings, etc.): 2-4 weeks notice

During holiday seasons, we recommend placing orders even earlier.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, we can accommodate various dietary restrictions including:

  • Gluten-free options
  • Vegan and vegetarian dishes
  • Nut-free preparations
  • Low-sugar alternatives

Please specify your dietary requirements when placing your order, and we'll do our best to accommodate your needs. Note that while we take precautions, our kitchen does process common allergens.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept the following payment methods:

  • All major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay and Google Pay
  • Cash on delivery

For catering and large orders, we may require a deposit at the time of booking.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy is as follows:

  • Regular orders: Full refund if cancelled at least 24 hours before scheduled delivery/pickup
  • Custom cakes: 50% refund if cancelled 48 hours before scheduled delivery/pickup
  • Catering orders: Refund policy depends on the size of the order and timing of cancellation

Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to cancel or modify your order.
